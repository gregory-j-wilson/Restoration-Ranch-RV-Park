About Us

Ramona, OK

Will & Jamie Wilson

Restoration Ranch RV Park was born from a simple dream — to create a place where the pace of life slows down, the stars shine bright, and every traveler feels right at home.

Will and Jamie Wilson personally oversee every corner of the park, ensuring each guest is welcomed with genuine Oklahoma hospitality. With generous spacing between sites, serenity isn't a selling point — it's just how things are out here.

Nestled close to Ramona, Oklahoma, the ranch sits amid rolling pastures and towering oaks — a true escape from the ordinary.

Community Garden

One of the ranch's most beloved features is its working community garden. Guests are invited to dig in, harvest fresh produce, and share in the simple joy of growing things from the earth.

Whether you're a seasoned green thumb or just curious, the garden is open to all — a gathering place that brings neighbors together under the Oklahoma sun.
